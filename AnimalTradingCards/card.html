Hummingbird

Hummingbirds hover in mid-air at rapid wing-flapping rates, typically around 50 times per second

  • Family: Trochilidae
  • Class: Reptilia
  • Average Length: 3 to 5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 3 to 5 years

They are known as hummingbirds because of the humming sound created by their beating wings which flap at high frequencies audible to humans.
